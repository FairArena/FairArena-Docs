## Notification Types

### System Notifications

Important platform messages:

**Examples:**
- "Welcome to FairArena!"
- "New feature available"
- "Scheduled maintenance"
- "Security alert"
- "Policy updates"

**Priority:** High - Read these carefully

### Invitation Notifications

Join requests and invites:

**Examples:**
- "You're invited to join [Team Name]"
- "You've been invited to [Project Name]"
- "[User] invited you to [Organization]"
- "Join request approved"

**Action Required:** Accept or decline invitations

### Mention Notifications

When someone tags you:

**Examples:**
- "@you mentioned you in a comment"
- "@you tagged in a discussion"
- "@you added to a thread"

**Stay Engaged:** Respond to mentions promptly

### Achievement Notifications

Celebrate your milestones:

**Examples:**
- "Your profile reached 100 views!"
- "You received your 10th star!"
- "Completed your profile - earned bonus credits"
- "One year on FairArena!"

**Feel Good:** Recognition for your engagement

### Update Notifications

Changes to your content:

**Examples:**
- "Role changed in [Team Name]"
- "Project [Name] updated"
- "Organization settings changed"
- "Your profile was featured"

**Stay Informed:** Know what's changing

### Alert Notifications

Urgent or important alerts:

**Examples:**
- "Unusual login detected"
- "Credit balance low"
- "Subscription expiring soon"
- "Action required"

**Take Action:** Address these promptly

### Follow Notifications

Activity from followed items:

**Examples:**
- "[Organization] posted an update"
- "[User] published new content"
- "Followed organization has a new team"

**Stay Updated:** Track what matters to you

### Star & Engagement

Social interactions:

**Examples:**
- "[User] starred your profile"
- "Someone viewed your profile"
- "New follower"
- "Organization you starred updated"

**Build Connections:** Engage with your community

## Common Questions

**Can I mute notifications temporarily?**

Yes! Enable "Do Not Disturb" mode in notification settings. Set for specific hours or until you turn it off.

**Will I miss important alerts if I unsubscribe?**

No. Critical security and account notifications always come through, even if you've opted out of everything else.

**Can I get notifications for specific teams only?**

Currently, notification settings are global. Team-specific settings are coming soon!

**How do I stop getting so many emails?**

Switch to daily digest or weekly summary mode instead of instant notifications.

**Why are my notifications delayed?**

Email delays can occur due to server load or your email provider. In-app notifications should be instant.

**Can I export my notification history?**

You can export your activity logs which include notification history. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Export Data.
