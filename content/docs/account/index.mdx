## Security Best Practices

<Steps>

### Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Secure your account with 2FA. Learn how in [Security & Privacy](/docs/account/security-and-privacy).

### Review Privacy Settings

Control who sees your information with [Privacy Controls](/docs/account/privacy-controls).

### Keep Information Updated

Regularly update your [Account Settings](/docs/account/account-settings) to ensure accuracy.

### Monitor Account Activity

Check your login history and active sessions regularly for suspicious activity.

</Steps>
