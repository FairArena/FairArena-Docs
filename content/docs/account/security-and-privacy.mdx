### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security:

**Why Enable 2FA?**
- Protects against password theft
- Adds second verification step
- Significantly reduces unauthorized access risk
- Peace of mind for your account

**How to Enable:**
1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Navigate to **Security**
3. Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
4. Scan QR code with authenticator app
5. Enter verification code
6. Save backup codes securely
7. 2FA is now active! ‚úÖ

**Supported Methods:**
- Authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy)
- SMS verification
- Email verification
- Backup codes

<Callout type="warning">
  Keep your backup codes in a safe place! You'll need them if you lose access to your 2FA device.
</Callout>
