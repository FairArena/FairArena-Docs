## Profile Visibility Settings

| Setting | Options | Who Can See |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Public Profile** | Enabled/Disabled | Everyone on internet |
| **Members Only** | Enabled/Disabled | FairArena members |
| **Organization Only** | Enabled/Disabled | Your org members |
| **Private** | Enabled/Disabled | Only you |

## Privacy Best Practices

<Callout type="tip" title="Regular Privacy Audits">
  Review your privacy settings quarterly to ensure they still match your goals.
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Start Conservative

Begin with private settings, then gradually open up as you're comfortable.

### Audit Regularly

- Check settings every 3 months
- Review connected apps
- Update preferences
- Remove old data

### Understand Trade-offs

**More privacy:**
- Less discoverability
- Fewer opportunities
- Limited networking

**More visibility:**
- Greater opportunities
- More connections
- Increased engagement

### Use Granular Controls

Don't just go all-or-nothing - use specific controls for different types of data.

</Steps>
