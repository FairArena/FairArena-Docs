## FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Authentication

**How do I reset my password?**

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to you.

**Why can't I log in?**

Check that you're using the correct email and password. Ensure caps lock is off. Try resetting your password if needed. Contact support if issues persist.

**Can I change my email address?**

Yes! Go to Account Settings and update your email. You'll need to verify the new email address.

**How do I delete my account?**

Navigate to Account Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Delete Account. Follow the prompts. Note there's a 30-day grace period.

### Profile & Privacy

**How do I make my profile public?**

Edit your profile, scroll to Privacy Settings, and select "Public Profile." Don't forget to save!

**Who can see my profile information?**

This depends on your privacy settings. You can choose Public (everyone), Private (FairArena members), or require authentication.

**Can I hide my profile from search engines?**

Private and authentication-required profiles aren't indexed by search engines. Public profiles may appear in search results.

**How do I enable profile view tracking?**

Edit your profile, find Privacy Settings, toggle "Track Profile Views" on, and save.

### Organizations & Teams

**How do I join an organization?**

Browse organizations, click the one you want to join, and click "Join" (if enabled) or request an invitation.

**Can I leave an organization?**

Yes, go to the organization page and select "Leave Organization" from settings. You'll lose access to all teams and projects.

**How do I create a team?**

Within an organization where you have permissions, click "Create Team" and fill in the details.

**What happens when I'm removed from a team?**

You lose access to the team and all its projects immediately. Your past contributions remain but you can't add new ones.

### Credits & Billing

**Do credits expire?**

No! Credits remain in your account indefinitely until you use them.

**How do I check my credit balance?**

View your balance on the dashboard or in Account Settings ‚Üí Credits.

**Can I get a refund?**

Yes, unused credits can be refunded per our refund policy. Submit a refund request from your transaction history.

**What payment methods do you accept?**

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, UPI (India), and other local payment methods.

**Where are my invoices?**

Access invoices from your transaction history or check your email after each purchase.

### Features & Usage

**How do I invite someone to a team?**

As a team admin, go to team settings, click "Invite Members," enter their email and role, and send the invitation.

**Can I customize roles and permissions?**

Yes! Organization, team, and project owners/admins can create custom roles with specific permissions.

**What's the difference between following and starring?**

Following means you'll get updates. Starring is a one-time recognition of appreciation.

**How do I export my data?**

Contact support to request a data export. We'll prepare and send you a complete copy of your data.

### Technical Issues

**The page won't load. What should I do?**

Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or trying a different browser. If it persists, contact support.

**I'm getting an error message. What does it mean?**

Take a screenshot of the error and submit a support request with details about what you were doing when it occurred.

**Why is the platform slow?**

This could be due to your internet connection, browser issues, or temporary server load. Try refreshing or clearing cache. Report persistent issues.

**Can I use FairArena on mobile?**

Yes! FairArena is responsive and works on mobile browsers. A dedicated app may come in the future.
