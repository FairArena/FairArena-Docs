## Refunds & Adjustments

### Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase:

**Eligible for Refund:**
- Credits not yet used
- Payment errors or duplicate charges
- Service issues preventing credit use
- Within refund window (check terms)

**Request a Refund:**
1. Go to your transaction history
2. Click on the transaction to refund
3. Select **Request Refund**
4. Provide reason for refund
5. Submit request
6. We'll review and respond within 2-3 business days

**Refund Process:**
- Unused credits are removed from your account
- Refund is processed to original payment method
- Typically takes 5-10 business days
- Email confirmation sent when complete

### Credit Adjustments

Sometimes we may adjust your credits:

- **Bonus Credits**: Promotional offers
- **Compensation**: If there's a service issue
- **Corrections**: Fixing errors or bugs
- **Rewards**: Special recognition or contests

All adjustments appear in your transaction history with clear descriptions.

<Callout type="info">
  Partial refunds are calculated based on unused credits. If you've used some credits, you'll be refunded for the remainder.
</Callout>

## Common Questions

**Do credits expire?**

No! Credits remain in your account indefinitely until you use them. There's no expiration date.

**Can I share credits with my team?**

Currently, credits are account-specific. For team usage, consider organization-level purchases (contact us for enterprise options).

**What happens to unused credits if I close my account?**

You can request a refund for unused credits when closing your account, subject to our refund policy.

**Can I gift credits to another user?**

Direct gifting isn't available yet, but we're working on it! Stay tuned for updates.

**Are there free credits for new users?**

Yes! New users often receive welcome credits. Check your dashboard after signing up.

**Can I get a refund after using some credits?**

Partial refunds are available for the unused portion, based on the package price breakdown.

**How do I know how many credits a feature uses?**

Feature credit costs are displayed before you use them. You'll always see the cost upfront.

**Can I pay monthly instead of buying credits?**

Currently, we use a credit system. Subscription plans may be available in the future.

**What payment currencies do you accept?**

We accept multiple currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, INR, and others depending on your region.

**Is my payment information secure?**

Absolutely! We use industry-leading security and don't store your complete card details on our servers.
