## Your Account Control Center

Account Settings is where you manage everything about your FairArena experience. From personal information to security settings, you have full control over your account.

## Accessing Account Settings

Find your settings easily:

1. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner
2. Select **Account Settings** from the dropdown
3. Or navigate directly from the dashboard

## Personal Information

### Basic Details

**Update Your Information:**
- First name and last name
- Email address (requires verification)
- Username
- Profile picture
- Contact information

**How to Update:**
1. Go to **Account Settings** ‚Üí **Personal Information**
2. Click **Edit** next to any field
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save Changes**

<Callout type="info">
  Email changes require verification. Check your new email for a confirmation link.
</Callout>

### Profile Settings

Quick access to profile management:

- Edit your full profile
- Update privacy settings
- Manage profile visibility
- Configure view tracking
- Update social links

## Security Settings

### Password Management

**Change Your Password:**
<Steps>

### Navigate to Security
Go to **Account Settings** ‚Üí **Security**

### Change Password
Click **Change Password**

### Enter Current Password
Verify your identity

### Create New Password
Enter strong, unique password

### Confirm New Password
Re-enter to confirm

### Save Changes
Your password is updated

</Steps>

**Password Tips:**
- Use at least 12 characters
- Mix uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include numbers and symbols
- Don't reuse passwords
- Consider using a password manager

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add extra security:

**Enable 2FA:**
1. Go to **Security Settings**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Choose your method (app/SMS)
4. Follow setup instructions
5. Save backup codes
6. 2FA is now active

**Manage 2FA:**
- View backup codes
- Regenerate codes
- Change 2FA method
- Disable 2FA (requires verification)

**Manage Sessions:**

- Force logout everywhere
- Review for suspicious activity

### Login History

Track your account access:

- View all login attempts

### Email Notifications

Control what emails you receive:

**Notification Types:**

**Configure Notifications:**

4. Save preferences

### Data Management

**Export Your Data:**
1. Go to **Privacy Settings**
2. Click **Export Data**

### Account Deletion

**Delete Your Account:**

<Callout type="warning">
  Account deletion is permanent. Make sure to export your data first!
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Access Deletion
Go to **Privacy Settings** ‚Üí **Delete Account**

### Review Warning
Understand what will be deleted

### Confirm Identity
Enter password or use 2FA

### Provide Reason
Optional feedback on why you're leaving

### Confirm Deletion
Acknowledge permanent removal

### Grace Period
30 days to change your mind

### Permanent Deletion
Account fully removed after grace period

</Steps>
