## üìö Core Features

### Build Your Professional Presence

Create a standout profile that showcases your skills, experience, and achievements. Control your privacy, track who views your profile, and get recognized with stars from the community.

**Learn More:** [User Profiles](/docs/user-profiles)

### Join Organizations

Connect with companies and professional communities. Organizations are where collaboration begins. Join existing organizations or create your own to bring people together.

**Learn More:** [Organizations](/docs/organizations)

### Collaborate in Teams

Teams are focused groups working toward common goals. Create teams within organizations, define roles, manage members, and organize your work effectively.

**Learn More:** [Teams & Projects](/docs/teams-and-projects)

### Manage Projects

Break down work into manageable projects. Control visibility, assign team members, track progress, and keep everything organized in one place.

**Learn More:** [Teams & Projects](/docs/teams-and-projects)

### Unlock Premium Features

Purchase credits to access enhanced features and capabilities. Flexible pricing plans fit every need, from individuals to large enterprises.

**Learn More:** [Credits & Pricing](/docs/credits-and-pricing)
