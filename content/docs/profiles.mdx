## Your Digital Identity

Your FairArena profile is your professional identity on the platform. It's how others discover you, understand your expertise, and connect with you for opportunities.

<Callout type="success" title="First Impressions Matter">
  Profiles with complete information receive **3x more views** and **5x more stars** than incomplete profiles!
</Callout>

## Profile Overview

Your profile consists of several key sections:

| Section | Purpose | Visibility |
|---------|---------|------------|
| **Basic Information** | Name, photo, bio | Always visible |
| **Professional Details** | Job title, company, experience | Controlled by privacy settings |
| **Skills & Interests** | Technical and soft skills | Controlled by privacy settings |
| **Experience** | Work history and achievements | Controlled by privacy settings |
| **Education** | Academic background | Controlled by privacy settings |
| **Social Links** | GitHub, LinkedIn, Portfolio | Controlled by privacy settings |
| **Certifications** | Professional certificates | Controlled by privacy settings |

## Creating Your Profile

<Steps>

### Add Profile Photo

1. Click your profile icon
2. Select **Edit Profile**
3. Click the camera icon or **Upload Photo**
4. Choose a professional photo (recommended: 400x400px)
5. Crop and position your image
6. Click **Save**

<Callout type="info">
  **Best practices:** Use a clear, professional headshot with good lighting. Avoid group photos, logos, or heavily filtered images.
</Callout>

### Write Your Bio

Your bio is your elevator pitch:

- Keep it under 500 characters
- Highlight your key expertise
- Mention what you're passionate about
- Include what you're currently working on

**Example Bio:**
```
Senior Full-Stack Developer with 8+ years building scalable web applications. 
Passionate about React, Node.js, and cloud architecture. Currently leading 
engineering at TechCorp and mentoring junior developers. Always learning!
```

### Configuring Privacy Settings

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Navigate to **Privacy**
3. Choose your **Profile Visibility**
4. Enable/disable **Require Authentication**
5. Enable/disable **Track Profile Views**
6. Click **Save Changes**

Stars are community recognition for impressive profiles:

**How it works:**

- Stars appear on your profile

- Increases your visibility

**View your stars:**

**Earn more stars by:**
- Completing your profile fully
- Adding quality content
- Being active in the community
- Showcasing impressive work
- Engaging with others

### Writing an Effective Bio

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Be specific about your expertise
- ‚úÖ Mention your current focus
- ‚úÖ Show personality while staying professional
- ‚úÖ Include what you're passionate about
- ‚úÖ Update regularly

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Use generic phrases like "hard worker"

### Showcasing Skills

**Organize skills by category:**

**Technical Skills:**
- Programming languages (Python, JavaScript, Java)
- Frameworks (React, Django, Spring Boot)
- Tools (Git, Docker, Kubernetes)
- Databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis)

**Professional Skills:**
- Leadership & Team Management
- Project Management
- Communication
- Problem Solving

**Tips for adding skills:**
- Be specific (React 19 instead of just JavaScript)
- Include proficiency levels when relevant
- Keep skills updated with your experience
- Remove outdated or irrelevant skills

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Can't upload profile photo">
    **Solutions:**
    - Ensure image is under 5MB
    - Use supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
    - Try a different browser
    - Clear browser cache
    - Compress image before upload
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Profile not showing in search">
    **Check:**
    - Profile visibility is set to Public or Private (not hidden)
    - Profile is at least 50% complete
    - Account is verified
    - Wait 24 hours for search index update
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't enable view tracking">
    **Requirements:**
    - Must enable "Require Authentication"
    - Need to have a verified email
    - Check privacy settings configuration
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Lost profile changes">
    **Prevention:**
    - Always click "Save Changes" before leaving
    - Don't use browser back button while editing
    - Save frequently when making many changes
    - Check for error messages before closing
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
