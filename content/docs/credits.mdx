## FAQs

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Do credits expire?">
    **No, never!** Once purchased, credits remain in your account until you use them. No expiration dates or time limits.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I transfer credits?">
    Credits are non-transferable between accounts for security reasons. However, organization owners can purchase credits for their organization that all members can use.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I don't use all my credits?">
    Keep them! They never expire. Use them whenever you're ready. There's no pressure or time limit.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">

  <Accordion title="Do you offer discounts?">

  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I know when I'm using credits?">
    You'll always see a confirmation before credits are deducted. The cost is displayed clearly, and you must confirm the action.

  <Accordion title="Can organizations share credits?">
