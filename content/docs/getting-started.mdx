## Quick Start

<Steps>

### Create Your Account

1. Visit the FairArena homepage
2. Click on **Sign Up** in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a secure password
4. Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to your inbox
5. Welcome aboard! üéâ

<Callout type="info">
  **Pro Tip:** Use a strong password with at least 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
</Callout>

### Complete Your Profile

Once you've signed up, it's time to make your profile shine:

<Tabs items={['Basic Info', 'Professional', 'Social Links']}>
  <Tab value="Basic Info">
    - Add a professional photo
    - Write a compelling bio (up to 500 characters)
    - Set your location and timezone
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Professional">
    - Add your skills and interests
    - Include your work experience
    - Add your education background
    - List certifications and achievements
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Social Links">
    - Connect your GitHub account
    - Link your LinkedIn profile
    - Add your Twitter/X handle
    - Include your personal website
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout type="success" title="Profile Visibility">
  A complete profile gets **3x more visibility** and helps others discover your expertise!
</Callout>

### Explore Organizations

Organizations are communities where professionals gather:

1. Go to the **Organizations** section
2. Browse available organizations
3. Click on any organization to view its details
4. Click **Join** to become a member (if open) or request an invite
5. Start exploring teams and projects within the organization

### Step 4: Join or Create a Team

Teams are where collaboration happens:

- **Join an existing team**: Browse teams within your organization and request to join
- **Create your own team**: If you have permission, create a team and invite members
- Set up your team profile with a description and logo
- Define roles and permissions for team members

### Step 5: Start Working on Projects

Projects help you organize work within teams:

1. Navigate to your team
2. Browse existing projects or create a new one
3. Add project details including description and goals
4. Invite team members to collaborate
5. Track progress and stay organized

</Steps>

- **Quick Actions**: Access frequently used features with one click
