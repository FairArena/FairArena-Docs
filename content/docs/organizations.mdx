## What are Organizations?

Organizations are communities where professionals and companies gather on FairArena. Think of them as your digital workplace or professional hub where teams, projects, and collaboration happen. Whether you're part of a company, open-source community, or professional group, organizations provide the structure for collaborative work.

## Finding Organizations

### Browse Organizations

**Public vs Private**
- **Public Organizations**: Visible to everyone and easier to join
- **Private Organizations**: More exclusive, require invitation or approval

## Joining an Organization

There are several ways to become part of an organization:

### Open Join Organizations

Some organizations allow anyone to join:

1. Find the organization you want to join
2. Click on the organization to view its profile
3. Look for the **Join** button
4. Click **Join** and you're in! üéâ

<Callout type="info">
  Organizations with "Join Enabled" allow instant access without approval.
</Callout>

### Invitation-Based Organizations

Most organizations work on an invite system:

**Receiving an Invite**
1. An organization member sends you an invite code
2. You'll receive the invite via email
3. Click the link in the email to accept
4. Choose your role (if multiple options are available)
5. Welcome to the organization!

**Invite Codes**
- Each invite code is unique and secure
- Codes can have expiration dates
- Codes may be role-specific
- Codes are single-use (can't be shared)

### Request Access

For closed organizations:

1. Visit the organization's profile
2. Click **Request Access** (if available)
3. Wait for an organization admin to review your request
4. You'll receive a notification when approved

### Following Organizations

Stay connected with organizations you're interested in:

- **Follow** organizations to get updates
- See followed organizations on your dashboard
- Get notified about important announcements
- Following doesn't make you a member (you still need to join)

**How to Follow**
1. Visit any public organization
2. Click the **Follow** button
3. You'll now see their updates
4. Unfollow anytime by clicking **Unfollow**

### Starring Organizations

Show appreciation for great organizations:

- **Star** organizations you find valuable
- Stars appear on the organization's profile
- Shows community appreciation
- Helps others discover quality organizations

**Starring vs Following**
- **Star**: One-time recognition ("I like this!")
- **Follow**: Ongoing connection ("Keep me updated!")

## Roles and Permissions

Organizations use role-based access control to manage what members can do:

### Common Roles

**Owner**
- Full control over the organization
- Can delete the organization
- Manage all settings and members
- Assign roles to others

**Admin**
- Manage organization settings
- Create and manage teams
- Invite and remove members
- Assign roles (except owner)
- Configure organization profile

**Member**
- Access organization content
- Join teams (if allowed)
- Participate in projects
- View organization information

**Viewer**
- Read-only access
- Can view organization content
- Cannot make changes
- Limited interaction

### Custom Roles

Organizations can create custom roles with specific permissions:

- View organization details
- Create teams
- Manage specific teams
- Invite members
- Manage projects
- View audit logs
- And more...

<Callout type="tip">
  Check with your organization admin to understand what permissions your role includes.
</Callout>

## Common Questions

**What's the difference between an organization and a team?**

Organizations are the top-level structure that contain multiple teams. Teams are groups within an organization that work on specific projects.

**Can I be part of multiple organizations?**

Yes! You can join as many organizations as you'd like and be active in all of them.

**How do I leave an organization?**

Go to the organization page, click on settings (if available), and select "Leave Organization". You'll be removed immediately.

**Can I create my own organization?**

Yes, any FairArena user can create an organization. Just navigate to the Organizations section and click "Create Organization".

**What happens if I'm removed from an organization?**

You'll lose access to all teams, projects, and organization content. You'll receive a notification when removed.

**Can I rejoin an organization after leaving?**

Yes, but you'll need a new invitation or the organization must have open joining enabled.

**Who can see private organizations?**

Only members of a private organization can view its details, teams, and projects.

**How many members can an organization have?**

There's no hard limit on organization membership. Organizations can scale based on your needs.
