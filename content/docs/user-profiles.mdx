## Your Professional Identity

Your profile is your digital identity on FairArena. It's how others discover you, understand your expertise, and connect with you. A well-crafted profile opens doors to opportunities and collaborations.

## Creating Your Profile

### Basic Information

When you first create your profile, you'll want to add these essentials:

**Profile Picture**
- Upload a professional, clear photo
- Use a high-quality image (recommended: 400x400px or larger)
- Make sure your face is clearly visible
- Avoid group photos or heavily filtered images

**Name**
- Add your first name and last name
- This helps others find and recognize you
- You can update this anytime from settings

**Bio**
- Write a compelling summary of who you are (up to 500 characters)
- Highlight your key skills and what you're passionate about
- Make it personal but professional
- Example: "Full-stack developer passionate about building scalable applications. Love working with React and Node.js. Always learning!"

### Professional Details

Make your profile complete by adding these details:

**Skills**
- List all your technical and professional skills
- Be specific (e.g., "React 19", "TypeScript", "Project Management")
- Add as many relevant skills as you have
- Skills help others find you when searching for expertise

**Interests**
- Share what topics you're interested in
- This helps connect you with like-minded professionals
- Can include both professional and personal interests

**Experience**
- Add your work history
- Include company names, roles, and what you accomplished
- List projects you've worked on
- Highlight your achievements

**Education**
- Add your educational background
- Include degrees, certifications, and courses
- Mention relevant training programs

**Languages**
- List languages you speak
- This helps in international collaboration

### Additional Information

Take your profile to the next level:

**Job Title**
- Your current role or designation
- Helps others understand your expertise level

**Company**
- Where you currently work
- Creates connections with others in your organization

**Location**
- Your city or country
- Useful for networking and opportunities

**Years of Experience**
- How long you've been in your field
- Gives context to your expertise

**Contact Information**
- Phone number (optional, shown only if you choose)
- Appears only to connections based on your privacy settings

**Portfolio & Social Links**
- Portfolio URL: Showcase your work
- GitHub: Share your code and contributions
- LinkedIn: Professional networking
- Twitter: Engage with the community

**Certifications & Awards**
- Add professional certifications you've earned
- List awards and recognitions
- Shows your commitment to excellence

**Date of Birth & Gender**
- Optional personal information
- Helps personalize your experience
- Controlled by privacy settings

**Resume**
- Upload your latest resume (PDF format recommended)
- Makes it easy for interested parties to learn more about you

## Privacy Settings

You have full control over who sees your profile information:

### Profile Visibility

**Public Profile**
- Anyone can view your profile, even without an account
- Great for maximum visibility and networking
- Recommended if you want to be discovered

**Private Profile**
- Only FairArena members can view your profile
- Offers more privacy while still being discoverable
- Good balance between visibility and privacy

### Authentication Requirement

**Require Authentication**
- Visitors must be logged in to view your profile
- Adds an extra layer of privacy
- Useful if you want to know who's viewing your profile

### View Tracking

**Track Profile Views**
- See who has viewed your profile
- Get insights into your profile's reach
- View statistics in the Profile Views section
- You can enable or disable this anytime

<Callout type="info">
  When view tracking is enabled, you'll get detailed information about who visited your profile, including their name, email, and when they viewed it.
</Callout>

## Profile Features

### Star System

The star system is a way for the community to show appreciation:

- Other users can **star** your profile if they find it impressive
- Stars appear on your profile and showcase your community recognition
- You can see who starred your profile in the **Profile Stars** section
- The more complete and engaging your profile, the more stars you're likely to receive

**View Your Stars**
1. Go to your profile
2. Click on **Profile Stars**
3. See everyone who has starred your profile
4. Each star includes the user's information and when they starred you

### Profile Views

Track your profile's visibility:

- See exactly who viewed your profile (when tracking is enabled)
- View timestamps of when your profile was accessed
- Understand which parts of your profile attract the most attention
- Access this information from the **Profile Views** section

**Understanding Your Views**
- Each view includes the viewer's name and email
- Views are recorded with timestamps
- Only unique views per user are counted
- You can see total view count at a glance

### Edit Your Profile

Keeping your profile updated is important:

1. Navigate to **My Profile**
2. Click the **Edit Profile** button
3. Update any information you want to change
4. Adjust your privacy settings
5. Click **Save** to apply your changes

<Callout type="tip">
  Update your profile regularly to reflect your latest skills, experiences, and achievements. Fresh profiles get more engagement!
</Callout>

## Profile Best Practices

### Make a Great First Impression

**Use a Professional Photo**
- Clear, well-lit, and recent
- Shows your face clearly
- Appropriate for a professional setting
- No logos or group photos

**Craft a Compelling Bio**
- Start with who you are and what you do
- Mention your key skills and expertise
- Add a touch of personality
- Keep it concise but informative

**Complete All Sections**
- Profiles with more information get more views
- Every section adds to your story
- Don't leave important sections empty

### Keep It Current

- Update your profile when you learn new skills
- Add new projects and experiences as they happen
- Refresh your bio periodically
- Update your photo every few years

### Show, Don't Just Tell

- Link to your actual work (portfolio, GitHub)
- Add specific examples in your experience section
- Quantify achievements when possible
- Let your work speak for itself

### Be Authentic

- Be honest about your skills and experience
- Write in your own voice
- Share what genuinely interests you
- Let your personality shine through

### Optimize for Discovery

- Use clear, searchable terms in your skills
- Include industry-standard terminology
- Add relevant keywords naturally in your bio
- Make it easy for others to find you

## Common Questions

**How do I make my profile stand out?**

Focus on completeness, authenticity, and value. Add a great photo, write a compelling bio, fill out all sections, link to your work, and keep everything updated.

**Who can see my profile?**

This depends on your privacy settings. You can choose to make it public (anyone), private (FairArena members only), or require authentication (logged-in users only).

**Can I see who viewed my profile?**

Yes! Enable "Track Profile Views" in your privacy settings, and you'll be able to see everyone who visits your profile.

**What's the difference between stars and views?**

Views are when someone looks at your profile. Stars are when someone actively chooses to recognize your profile as impressive or valuable. Stars are more meaningful than views.

**Can I hide certain information?**

Yes, many fields are optional. Only add information you're comfortable sharing. You can also adjust privacy settings to control visibility.

**How often should I update my profile?**

Update it whenever something significant changes - new job, new skills, completed projects, etc. A good practice is to review your profile every 3-6 months.

**Can I delete my profile?**

Yes, you can delete your account through your account settings. This will remove all your profile information from the platform.
