## Joining a Team

| Team Type | Access Method | Timeline |
|-----------|--------------|----------|
| **Open Teams** | Click to join | Immediate |
| **Request-Based** | Apply to join | 1-3 days |
| **Invite-Only** | Wait for invitation | Varies |

<Steps>

### Find Teams

**Within your organizations:**
1. Navigate to **Teams** section
2. Browse available teams
3. Filter by activity or focus area
4. View team profiles

### Review Team Details

Check these key points:
- Team description and goals
- Current member count
- Active projects
- Required skills or experience
- Team visibility settings

### Request to Join

For open or request-based teams:

<Tabs items={['Open Team', 'Request-Based']}>
  <Tab value="Open Team">
    1. Click **Join Team**
    2. Instant access granted
    3. Introduce yourself
    4. Start contributing
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Request-Based">
    1. Click **Request to Join**
    2. Explain your interest/skills
    3. Wait for team lead approval
    4. Receive notification
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

</Steps>

## Team Roles & Permissions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Team Owner">
    **Full control:**
    - Create and delete team
    - Manage all settings
    - Add/remove members
    - Assign roles
    - Delete projects
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Team Admin">
    **Management access:**
    - Edit team settings
    - Invite members
    - Create projects
    - Manage permissions
    - Cannot delete team
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Team Member">
    **Standard access:**
    - View team content
    - Join projects
    - Contribute work
    - Comment and collaborate
    - View member profiles
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Guest">
    **Limited access:**
    - View public content only
    - No editing permissions
    - Cannot join projects
    - Read-only access
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Communication Best Practices

<Steps>

### Stay Active

Engage regularly with your team:
- Check updates daily
- Respond to mentions promptly
- Share progress on your work
- Ask questions when stuck

### Use Clear Communication

**Effective messaging:**
- Be concise and specific
- Provide context
- Use @mentions for direct attention
- Include relevant links or files

### Provide Constructive Feedback

When reviewing others' work:
- Be specific and actionable
- Focus on the work, not the person
- Suggest improvements
- Acknowledge good work

### Respect Time Zones

<Callout type="info">
  Team members may be in different time zones. Be mindful of response time expectations.
</Callout>

</Steps>

## Conflict Resolution

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Communication Issues">
    **If misunderstandings occur:**
    - Address directly and privately
    - Assume good intentions
    - Seek clarification
    - Escalate to team lead if needed
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Work Disagreements">
    **When approaches differ:**
    - Present data and reasoning
    - Listen to other perspectives
    - Find common ground
    - Let team lead decide if stuck
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Workload Imbalance">
    **If work isn't distributed fairly:**
    - Track contributions openly
    - Discuss with team lead
    - Redistribute tasks
    - Set clear expectations
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Tips for Remote Collaboration

<Callout type="tip" title="Async-First">
  Document everything! Great documentation enables async work and helps new members onboard.
</Callout>

### Documentation Essentials

- **Project goals** and success criteria
- **Task assignments** and deadlines
- **Decision history** and rationale
- **Progress updates** regularly
- **Learnings and blockers** transparently

## Leaving a Team

Sometimes you need to move on:

<Steps>

### Assess the Decision

Consider if it's the right time:
- Complete your current commitments
- Notify team lead in advance
- Document your work for handoff

### Graceful Exit

1. Finish ongoing tasks
2. Transfer knowledge
3. Update project documentation
4. Leave team through settings
5. Stay connected with key members

</Steps>
