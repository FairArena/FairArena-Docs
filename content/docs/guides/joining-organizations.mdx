## Organization Types

| Type | Description | Membership | Best For |
|------|-------------|------------|----------|
| **Public** | Open to everyone | Instant join | Broad communities |
| **Request-Only** | Requires approval | Application needed | Curated groups |
| **Private** | Invite-only | Invitation required | Exclusive networks |
| **Internal** | Company staff only | Auto-approved for employees | Corporate teams |

## Best Practices

<Steps>

### Start Small

Join a few organizations first, get involved, then expand.

### Participate Actively

Don't just join - contribute! Comment, star projects, and engage with members.

### Check Activity Levels

Look for organizations with recent activity - check when the last project or team was created.

### Read Guidelines

Many organizations have specific rules or expectations - review them before joining.

### Build Your Reputation

Quality participation in fewer organizations beats passive membership in many.

</Steps>

## Managing Multiple Organizations

| Activity | Recommended Frequency |
|----------|----------------------|
| **Check updates** | Daily for active orgs |
| **Review new projects** | Weekly |
| **Engage with teams** | 2-3 times per week |
| **Update preferences** | Monthly |
| **Audit memberships** | Quarterly |

<Callout type="tip" title="Stay Organized">
  Use notifications wisely - enable for key organizations, mute less active ones.
</Callout>
