## Building Your Professional Profile

Your profile is your digital identity on FairArena. Make it count!

<Callout type="success" title="Impact of Complete Profiles">
  Profiles with photos and bios receive **5x more engagement** than incomplete ones.
</Callout>

## Profile Essentials

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Profile Photo">
    **Best practices:**
    - Professional headshot
    - Clear, well-lit image
    - Neutral background
    - Face clearly visible
    - Current photo (within 2 years)
    
    **File requirements:**
    - Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
    - Size: Max 5MB
    - Dimensions: At least 400x400px
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Display Name">
    **Guidelines:**
    - Use your real name or professional alias
    - 2-50 characters
    - No special characters except hyphens
    - Can be changed anytime
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Bio">
    **Crafting your bio:**
    - 2-3 compelling sentences
    - Highlight key expertise
    - Mention interests or goals
    - Max 500 characters
    - Update regularly
    
    **Example:**
    > "Full-stack developer passionate about AI and open source. Building scalable web applications at TechCorp. Always learning, always shipping."
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
